## üñ±Ô∏è How to Use the Gizmo

### Step 1: Hover Over an Arrow
- Move your mouse cursor over one of the three colored arrows on the cube
- The arrow will turn **YELLOW** and become **THICKER** when you hover over it
- **Red arrow** = Move along X axis (left/right)
- **Green arrow** = Move along Y axis (up/down)
- **Blue arrow** = Move along Z axis (forward/back)

### Step 2: Click and Hold
- **Left-click** on the highlighted (yellow) arrow
- **Hold** the left mouse button down

### Step 3: Drag to Move
- While holding the mouse button, **move your mouse**
- The cube will follow your mouse movement along the selected axis
- The arrow stays **yellow** while dragging

### Step 4: Release to Finish
- **Release** the left mouse button
- The cube stays at its new position
- The arrow returns to its original color
